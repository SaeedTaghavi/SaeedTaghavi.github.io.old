About me

I am a PhD student at the Physics Department of Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences. I am also a junior web developer.

From the research point of view, I am broadly interested in analyzing complex networks, and dynamical systems. I am also interested in devising computational methods for analyzing social, economical and biological systems. I graduated in 2014 with a MSc in Physics (Astrophysics) from the Physics Department of the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences, where I had the honor of being advised by Dr. Yousef Azizi. My BS degree is from the Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. You can find more about me in the following links:
